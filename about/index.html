Greetings!

I am Abhraneel. I hail from a small sleepy hamlet in the easternmost region of India called Digboi. (You may have heard of it - it is home to the oldest refinery in Asia and is currently the oldest functioning refinery in the world!)

I am currently enrolled Masters of Science in Information program at the University of Michigan at Ann Arbor, specialising in HCI.

I like to believe that I am a very curious person and this has led me to explore and develop a keen interest towards research. I am quite fascinated by the way we, as users, interact with and interpret Information, and how this process can be made more efficient. Thus I am fascinated in the field of Information and Data Visualization and would love opportunities for research in this domain. Other more general topics that I get excited about include Mathematics, Physics, Astronomy, Psychology and a host of other things.

I am an avid reader of books and I do not restrict myself to any particular genres. Although growing up I was always more inclined towards science fiction and high fantasy, my choices have matured gradually. Recently, I finished the Speaker For The Dead by Orson Scott Card, and I am currently reading Love & Math by Edward Frenkel. (I am soon going to upload my entire reading list, which would define me much better).

Academic interests aside, I play Football and Tennis regularly (but not as often as I would like). I am also a lifelong Manchester United suppoter. GGMU!
